GST-Compliant Invoicing

Generate professional, GST-compliant PDF invoices in one click. The system automatically determines whether to split as CGST+SGST (intra-state) or apply IGST (inter-state) by comparing your seller GSTIN with the buyer's GSTIN prefix.

  • Auto CGST/SGST and IGST calculation based on GSTINs
  • Support for all GST slabs: 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28%
  • HSN/SAC code support on every line item
  • Professional PDF layout with your business branding
  • Sequential invoice numbering (INV-0001, INV-0002...)
  • Email PDF invoices directly to customers (Pro+)

Real-Time Dashboard

Get a live pulse on your business. The dashboard streams metrics via Server-Sent Events (SSE) — revenue, pending invoices, stock levels, and top customers refresh automatically without you lifting a finger.

  • Revenue, invoice, and product metrics at a glance
  • Server-Sent Events (SSE) for live updates — no polling
  • Top customers by revenue
  • Low-stock alerts visible on the dashboard
  • Overdue invoice tracking
  • Comparative period stats (this month vs last month)

Inventory Control

Never run out of stock unexpectedly. Set minimum thresholds per product, receive automatic alerts when stock falls below the threshold, and view a complete log of every stock movement with full traceability.

  • Per-product stock thresholds with automatic alerts
  • Stock deducted automatically when invoice is created
  • Manual stock adjustments with reason logging
  • Complete stock movement history for every product
  • Multi-category product organisation
  • HSN/SAC code and GST rate per product

Role-Based Access Control

Give every team member exactly the right access. InvoBill has 5 built-in roles with granular module-level permissions — so your accountant can see financials without touching inventory, and staff can create invoices without deleting records.

  • 5 roles: Super Admin, Admin, Manager, Accountant, Staff
  • Granular view/create/edit/delete per module
  • Sidebar and action buttons adapt per role
  • Admin can reset any user's password
  • All actions recorded in the audit log
  • Session-based auth with bcrypt + CSRF protection

Payments & Credit Notes

Track every rupee. Record partial or full payments against invoices, issue credit notes for returns or adjustments, and always know your outstanding balance at a glance. All transactions are linked to invoices and customers.

  • Record partial and full payments against invoices
  • Multiple payment modes: cash, UPI, NEFT, cheque
  • Issue credit notes for returns and adjustments
  • Outstanding balance tracking per customer
  • Payment history and receipt generation
  • Overdue invoice detection and alerts

Under the hood

Built for speed & reliability

Chosen for reliability, speed, and simplicity — not hype.

Backend Go 1.25

Standard library net/http — no frameworks, just fast, compiled Go. Handles thousands of requests per second on minimal hardware.

Frontend HTMX 2.0

Hypermedia-driven UI. Partial page swaps without JavaScript frameworks. Fast, accessible, works without JS for core flows.

Database MySQL

Raw SQL queries via go-sql-driver/mysql. Each model owns its own migration. No ORM overhead, full control.

PDF go-pdf/fpdf

Server-side GST invoice PDF generation. No browser, no headless Chrome, no dependencies. Pure Go, always consistent output.

Security bcrypt + CSRF

Password hashing with bcrypt, 64-char hex session tokens, CSRF protection on all forms, rate limiting at 60 rps.

Real-time Server-Sent Events

Live dashboard metrics streamed via SSE — no WebSocket complexity, no polling, just efficient one-way real-time data.
