Our story

Built for Indian businesses

InvoBill was created to give small and medium Indian businesses a professional, GST-compliant billing and inventory platform — without the bloat or the enterprise price tag.

What drives us

Our mission & values

Speed first

Built on Go's standard library and HTMX — zero JavaScript frameworks, instant page loads, lightweight footprint. Your time is money.

GST compliance

India-first design. CGST + SGST for intra-state, IGST for inter-state — auto-calculated based on GSTIN prefixes. No manual tax math.

Security built-in

Session-based auth with bcrypt, CSRF protection on every form, rate limiting at 60 rps, and role-based access across all modules.

Simple by design

How InvoBill works

Three steps from signup to your first GST invoice.

1

Create your account

Register in seconds. Set up your seller GSTIN, business name, and address. Invite your team with appropriate roles.

2

3

Under the hood

Technology stack

Chosen for reliability, speed, and simplicity — not hype.

Backend Go 1.25

Standard library net/http — no frameworks, just fast, typed, compiled Go with instant startup.

Frontend HTMX 2.0 + Vanilla JS

Hypermedia-driven UI. Partial page swaps, live updates via SSE — zero JavaScript framework bloat.

Database MySQL 8+

Raw SQL via go-sql-driver/mysql. Schema auto-migrates on startup. Each store owns its own schema.

PDF go-pdf/fpdf

Server-side GST invoice PDF generation with CGST/SGST/IGST layout — no browser, no headless Chrome.

Auth & Security bcrypt · sessions · CSRF

Bcrypt password hashing, MySQL session tokens, CSRF on every form, 60 rps rate limiting, panic recovery.

Templates html/template (SSR)

Go's built-in XSS-safe templating. Parsed from disk on every request — HTML changes are live without restart.

Charts Chart.js 4

Interactive bar charts, doughnut charts, and sparklines on reports and dashboard pages.

PWA Service Worker

Installable as an app on Android and desktop. Offline shell with cache-first asset serving.
